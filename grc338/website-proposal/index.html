Introduction

Metropolitan Ice Cream Club

Metro Club is an ice cream subscription service which provides vegan and dairy free ice cream deliveries for all ice cream lovers, particularly catering to customers with dietary preferences, which makes it hard for them to shop and find delicious ice creams which fit their diet in a usual grocery store.

Target audience

As stated in the intro, Metro Club was made for all ice cream lovers. Our services and products are particularly desireable to ice cream lovers with vegan or dairy free diets.

Shopping for dairy free and vegan ice cream in grocery stores can be hard. Many don't offer the options that our customers are looking fo and leaves them empty handed. Metro Club brings the convenience of traditional dairy ice cream to dairy-free customers door.

Our Scoop

So what's the scoop? Metropolitan Ice Cream Club started because we know you're tired of having to go shop around the grocery store just to settle for a less than satisfactory icecream, or one that doesn't fit with your dietary restrictions. Why not bring the perfect ice cream right to your door? Unlike similar brands, we don't like diet culture, we aren't trying to make ice cream "skinny" we just want ice cream to be able to be enjoyed by everyone. As they say... I scream, you scream, we all scream for ice cream! Metro Club hears you, and we have you covered!
